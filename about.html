Savour the Sea, One Bite at a Time

Welcome to Kuttimulla Restaurant, where the essence of Kochi's rich culinary heritage comes alive on your plate. Nestled in the heart of Kochi, we specialise in serving the freshest and most delectable seafood, crafted with love and tradition. At Kuttimulla, we believe that food is more than just a meal—it's an experience. Our chefs are passionate about bringing you the finest flavours from the sea, using locally sourced ingredients and age-old recipes passed down through generations. Whether you're a seafood connoisseur or trying it for the first time, our dishes are sure to tantalise your taste buds and leave you craving more. Join us at Kuttimulla Restaurant for an authentic dining experience that celebrates the vibrant culture and flavours of Kochi. We look forward to serving you a taste of the sea, right here in our cosy and welcoming restaurant.
